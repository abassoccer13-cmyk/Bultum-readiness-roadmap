New for 2026–27: Tier 2 literacy supervision

Beginning this school year, any paraprofessional, tutor, or volunteer who delivers Tier 2 reading intervention must be supervised by a licensed teacher who has completed an MDE-approved evidence-based reading course and the READ Act training developed by CAREI / the Regional Literacy Network. Decide who that supervisor is early — it gates your whole intervention block. MDE intervention guidance →
